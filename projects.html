Aegean Airlines Review

With this mini review, it is supposed to be presented some of my skills with datasets and data vizualization techniques. Always using Jupyter Notebook, Python, Pandas and some amazing libraries and modules for my plots such as MatPlotLibm Seaborn and Plotly.
I compared the Greek Aegean-Airlines with other airline companies, with the intension of reaching to valuable conclusions.
For this project are used free datasets from Skytrax. After cleaning and formating the appropriate data frames, i proceeded to vizualization trying to understand, depending on people opinion, the status of Aegean-Airlines.
